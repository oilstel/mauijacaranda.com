Maui Jacaranda

Season Map Guide

The jacarandas are currently in bloom on Maui.

Each spring the non-native jacaranda trees bloom. The bloom's hotspot is in Kula, on the slopes of Haleakala. If the background of this website is violet then the jacarandas are blooming. Use this map to visit a tree near you.

If you are on Maui, you might want to visit The Curtis Wilson Cost Gallery in upcountry Maui. Curtis has been documenting the seasonal jacarandas in his oil paintings since 1973.
